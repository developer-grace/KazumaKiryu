Kazuma Kiryu

Kazuma Kiryu is a fictional character and a former primary protagonist in the Sega video game series Like A Dragon, formerly known as Yakuza.

He is the fourth chairman of the Tojo Clan, the legendary "Dragon of Dojima" (堂島の龍 Dōjima no Ryū) and owner of Morning Glory Orphanage.
